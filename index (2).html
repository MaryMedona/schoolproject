St.Xavier School


St. Xavier’s School, Krishnai is a KG to class 12 co-educational day and boarding school affiliated to the CBSE, New Delhi. At Senior Secondary level, we offer SCIENCE & COMMERCE stream with all the modern facilities. St. Xavier’s School was founded by Mr Azizul Hussain primarily to cater to the educational needs of boys and girls of Krishnai and its surrounding rural areas. We are located near the Krishnai town, opposite of Krishnai Police Station. Established in the year 2002 December to enable it to conform to the standards and requirements of modern residential education; it is to incorporate the healthier features of the English Schools and to integrate that education with the Competitive environment of modern education. At SXSK we are committed to build a caring and supportive community in which every child can flourish. SXSK academic programme offers a strong and broad foundation in the field of education. We believe in an all-round education and to bring out the best in every individual. School’s extensive curriculum along with its varied approach to teaching, not only prepare our students for good tertiary education but also instils in them a lifelong love of learning.e.
